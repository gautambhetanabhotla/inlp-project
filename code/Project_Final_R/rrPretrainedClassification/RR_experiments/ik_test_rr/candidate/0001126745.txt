RatioOfTheDecision	6.
RatioOfTheDecision	Validity of plural remedies, if available under the law, cannot be doubted.
RatioOfTheDecision	If any standard book on the subject is examined, it will be found that the debate is directed to the application of the principle of election, where two or more remedies are available to a person.
RatioOfTheDecision	Even if the two remedies happen to be inconsistent,they continue for the person concerned to choose from, until he elects one of them, commencing an action accordingly.
