RatioOfTheDecision	We are asked to believe that <NAME> on being seen by the <ORG> party in the waste lauds of Patkul immediately ran away, was pursued and captured ; that he forthwith made a confession and showed some stolen orna ments buried near the particular spot where he was seized.
