RatioOfTheDecision	Lastly, the <ORG> cannot supplement reasons for urgency through counter affidavit when documents showing such urgency as described in paragraph No.

RatioOfTheDecision	, <ORG> has observed that when statutory functionaries makes an order based on certain grounds, its validity has to be judged on the basis of the material and the reasons on the record and they cannot be permitted to supplement fresh reasons in the shape of affidavit or otherwise.
RatioOfTheDecision	Therefore, the plea of the <ORG> that there as real urgency for the reasons that an Olympic size Stadium is to be constructed is not tenable and is not acceptable.
