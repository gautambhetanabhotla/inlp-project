Fact	A careful examination of the facts, lead to the following inferences :  i) All allegations (relating to petitioner and his family members being taken to <ORG> Stations/<ORG> Posts and being questioned/beaten up/tortured) are in connection with the effort of <ORG> to find the whereabouts of <NAME>, whenever he was involved in a serious incident, that is (a) incident on 10.3.1998 when <NAME> was suspected of killing a <ORG> constable, (b) incident on 25.1.2001 when <NAME> escaped from <ORG> custody when he was being taken to court, and (c) incident on 13/14.2.2001 when <NAME> was suspected of killing two persons at Tohana.
Fact	ii) Though there is some evidence of illegal detention and beating of petitioner and his relatives, the allegations of custodial torture are exaggerated and to a certain extent false.
Fact	iii) There is n o medical evidence nor any visible scars/ marks/disability resulting from the alleged torture, either in the case of petitioner or his family members/relatives.
Fact	iv) The complaints of petitioner and his relatives are against different police officers of different police stations (totally unconnected with each other) in regard to incidents at different points of time, in March, 1998, April, 1998, July, 1998, January, 2001, February, 2001 and June, 2001.  v) The case of Petitioner is that he and/or his relatives were harassed, illegally confined, or tortured, to find out the whereabouts of <NAME>.
Fact	The police contend that the allegations by petitioner and his relatives, are by way of a well conceived plot to prevent police investigation in regard to misdeeds by <NAME> and his associates and to pre-empt any action by the police against <NAME> or his family members.
