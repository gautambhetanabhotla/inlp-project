RatioOfTheDecision	It is no doubt true that no sufficient material is available at this stage to substantiate that the goods are being sold by the plaintiff in Delhi, but this matter would be gone into at the time of the trial of the suit.
RatioOfTheDecision	At this stage, it is sufficient that the specific averment in the plaint, whatever it may be worth, has been made that the plaintiff and defendant are selling the goods within the territorial jurisdiction of Delhi.
RatioOfTheDecision	Therefore, in my opinion this court has territorial jurisdiction to try this suit.
RatioOfTheDecision	(9) In order to succeed in the  injunction application, three factors must be kept in view.
RatioOfTheDecision	that is, the establishment of prima facie case, the balance of convenience between the parties and in case injunction is not granted this will cause irreparable in Jury to the petitioner.
RatioOfTheDecision	In order to show prima facie case, the plaintiff's counsel has referred to the wrappers of the plaintiff and the defendant placed on the record and strenuously urged that having regard to the column, scheme, get up, lay out, style and arrangement of words the wrappers used by the plaintiff and defendant are strikingly deceptively similar and thereby the defendant is guilty of passing its inferior quality of goods to the buyers as of the plaintiff.
RatioOfTheDecision	There is good deal of force in this argument.
RatioOfTheDecision	Having regard to the deceptive similarity between the' get up, scheme, column, ink etc.
RatioOfTheDecision	of two competing wrappers gives the wrapper of defendant of overall effect of deceptive similarity which prima facie constitute infringement of copyright of the plaintiff.
RatioOfTheDecision	The comparative de scription of the wrapper set out in the plaint which is amply reflected on a visual examination of the competing wrappers leaves absolutely no manner of doubt that the wrapper of defendant is deceptively similar to the wrapper extensively used by the plaintiff since 1978.
RatioOfTheDecision	The deceptive similarity is likely to cause confusion in the course of trade, particularly having regard to the class of people, ladies and domestic servants which constitute the buying public, in relation to washing soap.
RatioOfTheDecision	Therefore, the plaintiff prima facie is entitled to protection at this stage.
RatioOfTheDecision	(10) The next question that arises for consideration at this stage is whether between the parties who has established the user of the trade mark and copyright at point of time irrespective of the ownership of such mark and copyright.
RatioOfTheDecision	In this connection there is a statement filed by the plaintiff from 1978- 87.
RatioOfTheDecision	The plaintiff has also filed <ORG> copies of the bills of sale of the soap of trade mark "BILLY" in the wrappers used by it from 19-1 0-1978 to 25-5-1987.
RatioOfTheDecision	A perusal of these documents clearly demonstrates that the plaintiff is selling the soap of trade mark "BILLY" with the distinctive artistic wrapper of "BILLY" from the year 1978 uptil now and continuing to sell the same under its trade mark and artistic wrapper.
RatioOfTheDecision	On the other hand, the defendant has admittedly claimed user of the trade mark "BILLY" with the device of "BILLY" in the identical carton from November, 1987 which is about 10 years later.
RatioOfTheDecision	This has been admitted by the defendant in paragraph 2 of the preliminary objections of the reply.
RatioOfTheDecision	The law is pretty well settled that in order to succeed at this.
RatioOfTheDecision	stage the plaintiff has to establish user of the aforesaid mark prior in point of time than the impugned user by the defendant.
RatioOfTheDecision	The plaintiff, therefore, is to succeed as he is prior user of the trade mark vis-a-vis the defeadant.
RatioOfTheDecision	(11) It was next submitted by counsel for the defendant that the plaintiff has no right to use the trade mark "BILLY" and artistic wrapper as so many othe r parties who are referred to earlier were also using the same trade mark.
RatioOfTheDecision	This contention of the defendant is wholly devoid of any force.
RatioOfTheDecision	In this connection it is sufficient to say that under the law it is the right of two parties before the court which has to be determined and, as already discussed, the plaintiff being prior user has the better and legal right to seek relief from this court.
RatioOfTheDecision	(12) In the light of what is discussed above, the prima facie case in favor of the plaintiff stands established.
RatioOfTheDecision	The use of the trade mark and artistic wrapper by the plaintiff much earlier to that of the defendant not only shows a prima facie case in plaintiff's favor but balance of convenience also lies in its favor.
RatioOfTheDecision	Furthermore, if the defendant firm is not restrained by means,' of interim injunction, it will continue to market its goods with the offending mark and wrapper with the result that the plaintiff will suffer irreparable loss and injury.
RatioOfTheDecision	(13) I would, therefore, grant an injunction restraining the defenda nt, during the pendency of the suit, from manufacturing, selling, offering for sale or otherwise dealing in the washing soap under the impugned trade mark "BILLY" and artistic wrapper of the plaintiff or under any other trade mark and artistic wrapper which may be similar and/or deceptively identical to the trade mark "BILLY" and artistic wrapper of the plaintiff and also from passing off its products of washing soap as and for the quality goods of the plaintiff under the trade mark "BILLY" and artistic wrappers which are quite similar and deceptively identical to the trades mark and artistic wrappers of the plaintiff.
