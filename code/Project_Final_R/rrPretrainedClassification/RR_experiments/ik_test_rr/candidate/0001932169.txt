Fact	PETITIONER: <NAME>, JABALPUR, M.P.
Fact	Vs. RESPONDENT: <NAME> & ORS.
Fact	DATE OF JUDGMENT: 07/04/1997 BENCH: <NAME>, D.P.
Fact	WADHWA ACT: HEADNOTE: JUDGMENT:  O R D E R Leave granted.
Fact	This appeal by special leave arises from the order of <ORG> passed in Misc.
Fact	Petition No.
Fact	2935 of 1986 on 9th February, 1995 and the order passed in Review petition (MCC No.
Fact	461 of 1995) dated 12th July, 1996.
Fact	On a Scheme sponsored by <ORG>, the appellant-institute had taken up the project.
Fact	As many as 625 posts were created in different scales.
Fact	The respondents while working as <NAME>, Machine Operators & Class IV employees respectively in the regular pay scales of Rs.750-945, filed a writ petition in <ORG> for regularisation of their service, by the impugned order, the high Court allowed the writ petition, feeling aggrieved,, the appellant-institute has filed this appeal by special leave.
Fact	It is not in dispute that the  <ORG> is sponsored by <ORG> and, therefore, permanent posts cannot be created.
Fact	The posts are co-terminus with the scheme.
Fact	On abolition of the <ORG>, posts also necessarily stand abolished.
Fact	We are informed that the <ORG> may continue to be in force; but it depends upon the <ORG> being sponsored and the posts made available by the <ORG>.
Argument	Therefor, the direction to regularise the services is violative of their right to posts.
Argument	The order of <ORG> to that extent is modified.
Argument	On whatever posts the respondents are working and discharging their duty, the scale of pay the said post is directed to be paid.
RulingByPresentCourt	The appeal is accordingly disposed of.
RulingByPresentCourt	No costs.
