RulingByLowerCourt	It is true that the effect of excluding the Secretaries from the office of <NAME> will be to render that part of the will that provides, that "there shall always be five shabaits including the Secretary," impossible of performance, but that I think is inevitable; if any particular number of persons are appointed in a will to fill certain offices, it is inevitable that that number must be reduced if for any reason the appointment of any one or more of such persons is void.
