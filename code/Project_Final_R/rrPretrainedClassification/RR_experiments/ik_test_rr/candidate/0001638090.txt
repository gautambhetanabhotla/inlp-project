Fact	7966-7967 of 1996 entitled <CITATION_535731> on December 17, 2002,  259 ITR 449.
Fact	6.
Fact	As against this, learned senior counsel appearing on behalf of the respondents submitted that in the judgments rendered by this court in <NAME>'s case [2001] 252 ITR 1 as well as in <ORG> arriers case [2003] 259 ITR 449, the court has not considered Explanation 1 under Section 234B of the Income-tax Act and, therefore, the said issue requires reconsideration.
RatioOfTheDecision	7.
RatioOfTheDecision	In our view, after the pronouncement of the judgment in <NAME>'s case , it is not open to us to reconsider the same as it has been specifically held that <ORG> has no power to waive mandatory interest as contemplated under Sections 234A , 234B and 234C of the Income-tax Act.
RatioOfTheDecision	8.
