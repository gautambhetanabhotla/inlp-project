RatioOfTheDecision	We have however, already said  earlier that, since the claimant was driving the tempo absolutely on the kachcha part of the road to his left and had also lowered the speed to 20 k. m. per hour, he had no reason to suspect that the person driving the truck shall come down on that part, of the road and dash against the tempo.
