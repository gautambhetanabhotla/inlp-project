RatioOfTheDecision	Section 46 has no application here and therefore we do not need to set out its pro visions.
RatioOfTheDecision	We are merely concerned with the proviso in Section 47 which plainly indicates that an arbitration award obtained otherwise than under the Act may with the consent of the parties interested in it be regarded as a compromise of a pending suit .
RatioOfTheDecision	There is nothing in the proviso which precludes antecedent consent.
