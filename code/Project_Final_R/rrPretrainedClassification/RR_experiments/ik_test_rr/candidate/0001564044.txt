RulingByPresentCourt	For the reasons given above these appeals must succeed and are hereby allowed.
RulingByPresentCourt	The entire acquisition proceedings in respect of the lands of the appellants in all the appeals are, hereby quashed.
RulingByPresentCourt	In matters of this nature we would have taken due notice of laches on the part of the appellants while granting the above relief but we are satisfied that so far as the present appellants are concerned they have not been guilty of laches, delay or acquiescence at any stage.
