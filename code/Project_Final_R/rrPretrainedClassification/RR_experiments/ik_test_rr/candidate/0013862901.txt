RatioOfTheDecision	There is by now, fairly abundant case law on the questions as to "minority",  the minority's right to "establish", and their right to "administer" educational institutions.
RatioOfTheDecision	These questions have arisen in regard to a variety of institutions all over the country.
RatioOfTheDecision	They have arisen in regard to Christians, Muslims and in regard to certain sects of Hindus and linguistic groups.
RatioOfTheDecision	The <ORG> in certain cases have accepted without much scrutiny the version of the claimant that the institution in question was founded by a minority community while in some cases the <ORG> have examined very minutely the proof of the establishment of the institution.
RatioOfTheDecision	It should be borne in mind that the words "establish" and "administer" used in Article 30(1) are to be read conjunctively.
RatioOfTheDecision	The right claimed by a minority community to administer the educational institution depends upon the proof of establishment of the institution.
