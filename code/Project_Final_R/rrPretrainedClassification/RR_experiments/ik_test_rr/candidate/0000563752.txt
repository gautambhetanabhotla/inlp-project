RatioOfTheDecision	There does not seem to tie any special significance for the word "supplies".
RatioOfTheDecision	If the supply of goods is for no consideration, then it cannot come within the sales-tax levy, as there is no element of sale.
RatioOfTheDecision	The transaction is essentially a gift.
