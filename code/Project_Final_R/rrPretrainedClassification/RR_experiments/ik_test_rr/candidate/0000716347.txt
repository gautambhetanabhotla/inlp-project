RatioOfTheDecision	It is admitted that no such regulations had been made by <ORG> by the time the impugned notifications were issued.
RatioOfTheDecision	<ORG> has negatived the above plea and according to us, rightly.
RatioOfTheDecision	It is true that section 79 (h) of the Act authorises <ORG> to make regulations laying down the principles governing the fixing of Grid Tariffs.
RatioOfTheDecision	But section 46 (1) of the Act does not say that no Grid Tariff can be fixed until such regulations are made.
RatioOfTheDecision	It only provides that the Grid Tariff shall be in accordance with any regulations, made in this behalf.
RatioOfTheDecision	That means that if there were any regulations the Grid Tariff should be fixed in  accordance with such regulations and nothing more.
RatioOfTheDecision	We are of the view that the framing of regulations under section 79 (h) of the Act cannot be a condition precedent for fixing the Grid Tariff.
RatioOfTheDecision	A similar contention was rejected by this Court in <CITATION_1100563> (l) which was a case arising under <ORG> Act , 1950.
