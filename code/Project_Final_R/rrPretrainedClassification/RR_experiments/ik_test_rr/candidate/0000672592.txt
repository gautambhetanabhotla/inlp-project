RatioOfTheDecision	Having given our careful consideration to the question, we are of the opinion that there is no anomaly and that there are no different periods of limitation for making deposits and/or filing an application for setting aside the sale.
RatioOfTheDecision	It is by virtue of Order XXI Rule 89 C.P.C.
RatioOfTheDecision	that an application for setting aside a sale and a deposit can be made.
