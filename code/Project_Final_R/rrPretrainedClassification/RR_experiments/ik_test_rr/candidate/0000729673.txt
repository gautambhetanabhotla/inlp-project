Fact	CRIMINAL COURTS ARE NORMALLY CONSTITUTED UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE CRIMINAL PROCEDURE CODE.
Fact	SECTION 6 OF THE CODE OF CRIMINAL PROCEDURE, 1973 (FOR SHORT, `THE CODE') PROVIDES FOR THE CLASSES OF CRIMINAL COURTS.
Fact	IN ADDITION TO THE PROVISIONS CONTAINED IN THE CODE OR THE OLD CODE OF 1898, FROM TIME TO TIME, ENACTMENTS HAVE BEEN PASSED PROVIDING THAT IN RESPECT OF CERTAIN OFFENCES, THERE WILL BE A SPECIAL COURT MANNED BY PERSONS HAVING SPECIFIED QUALIFICATIONS.
Fact	IN THE PRESENT APPEALS, WE ARE CONCERNED WITH SUCH AN ENACTMENT, NAMELY, THE SPECIAL COURT (TRIAL OF OFFENCES RELATING TO TRANSACTIONS IN SECURITIES) ACT, 1992 (`THE ACT' FOR SHORT).
Fact	THE PASSING OF THE ACT WAS PRECEDED BY AN ORDINANCE WHICH WAS PROMULGATED ON 6TH JUNE, 1992.
Fact	IT IS AN ACT TO PROVIDE FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A SPE CIAL COURT FOR THE TRIAL OF OFFENCES RELATING TO TRANSACTIONS IN SECURITIES AND FOR MATTERS CONNECTED THEREWITH OR INCIDENTAL THERETO.
Fact	IN THE YEAR 1992, LARGE SCALE IRREGULARITIES AND MALPRACTICES WERE NOTICED BY <ORG> IN RELATION TO TRANSACTIONS IN BOTH THE GOVERNMENT AND OTHER SECURITIES, INDULGED IN BY SOME BROKERS IN COLLUSION WITH THE BANKS AND FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS.
Fact	THE SAID IRREGULARITIES AND MALPRACTICES LED TO THE DIVERSION OF FUNDS FROM BANKS AND FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS TO THE INDIVIDUAL ACCOUNTS OF CERTAIN BROKERS.
Fact	THE ACT DEALS WITH THE SITUATION AND IN PARTICULAR TO ENSURE SPEEDY RECOVERY OF THE HUGE AMOUNT INVOLVED, TO PUNISH THE GUILTY AND RESTORE CONFIDENCE IN AND MAINTAIN THE BASIC INTEGRITY AND CREDIBILITY OF THE BANKS AND FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS.
