Fact	Article 32 is designed for the enforcement of Fundamental Rig hts of a citizen by <ORG>.
Fact	It provides for an extra-ordinary remedy to safeguard the fundamental rights of a citizen.

Argument	These coal mines and collieries are known as West Bokaro <ORG> and the <ORG> have two coal washeries where the coal after its extraction from the mines is brought and broken into graded pieces and there-  9  after it is processed for the purpose of reducing its ash contents.
Fact	A chemical process is carried out which is known as `froth floatation process'.
Fact	Under this process the graded coal is mixed with diesel oil, pine oil and many other chemical ingredients and thereafter it is washed with the lacs of gallons of water.
Fact	The end water is washed coal with reduced quantity of ash content fit for high graded metallurgical process for the purposes of manufactu re of steel.

Fact	Along with the discharged water, small particles of coal are carried away to the pond where the coal particles settle down on the surface of the pond, and the same is collected after the pond is de-watered.
Fact	The coal particles which are carried away by the water is called the slurry which is ash free, it contains fine quality of coal which is used as fuel.
