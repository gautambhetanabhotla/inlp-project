Argument	Learned counsel for the respondent has placed reliance on a decision of this court in <ORG> v. <NAME> 1974 WLN (UC) 365 wherein it has been held that if a landlord is occupying another premises on rent, it cannot be said that his requirement is not reasonable or bonafide.

RatioOfTheDecision	On the other hand, taking another sh op on rent by the landlord during the pendency of litigation, shows the bonafide of the landlord and genuineness of his use that the suit premises were required by him reasonable and bonafide for carrying on business by his son.
