Fact	After considering the decisions cited before me on behalf of the parties, it appears to me that the said question was decided by the <ORG> decision of <ORG> reported in AIR 1935 Mad 365 ( <CITATION_1981980> .
Precedent	).
Precedent	It was held that on the restoration of the suit, all ancillary orders are restored without any further orders.
Precedent	When an order dismissing a suit for default is set aside on an application for that purpose, the suit remains as it was on the date it was dismissed, and all proceedings taken up to that date must be deemed to be in force when the dismissal is set aside.
