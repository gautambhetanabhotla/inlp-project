Precedent	Section 23 of the Land Acquisition Act provides that in mining the amount of compensation to, be awarded for the land acquired under the Act, the <ORG> shall take into.

RatioOfTheDecision	The market value means the price that a willing purchaser would pay to a willing seller for the property having due regard to its existing condition with all its existing advantages and its potential possibilities when laid out in the most advantageous manner excluding any advantage due to the carrying out of the scheme for which the property is compulsorily acquired.
RatioOfTheDecision	In considering market value the disinclination of the vendor to part with his land and the urgent necessity of the purchaser to buy should be disregar ded.
RatioOfTheDecision	There is an element of guess-work inherent in most cases involving determination of the market value of the acquired land.
RatioOfTheDecision	But this in the very nature of things cannot be helped.
