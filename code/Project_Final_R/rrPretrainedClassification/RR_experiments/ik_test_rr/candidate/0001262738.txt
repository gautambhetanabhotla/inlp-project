RatioOfTheDecision	The word "removal" used synonymously with the term "dis- missal" generally implies that the Officer is regarded as in some manner blameworthy or deficient.
RatioOfTheDecision	The action of removal is founded on some ground personal to the officer and there is a levelling of some imputation or charge against him.
RatioOfTheDecision	But there is no such element of charge or imputation in the case of compulsory retirement.
RatioOfTheDecision	In other words a compulsory retirement does not involve any stigma or implication of misbehaviour or incapacity.
RatioOfTheDecision	Dismissal or removal is a punishment and involves loss of benefit already earned.
Fact	The Officer, dismissed or removed, does not got pension which he has earned.
Fact	On compulsory retirement  the Officer will be entitled to the pension that he has actually earned and there is no diminution of the accrued benefit.
