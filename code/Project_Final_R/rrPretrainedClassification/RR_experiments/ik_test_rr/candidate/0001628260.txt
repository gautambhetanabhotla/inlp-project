Precedent	The compensation is in the nature of exempellary damages' awarded against the wrong doer for the breach of its public law duty and is independent of the rights available to the aggrieved party to claim compensation under the private law in an action based on tort, through a suit instituted in  a court of competent jurisdiction or/and persecute the offender under the penal law.
