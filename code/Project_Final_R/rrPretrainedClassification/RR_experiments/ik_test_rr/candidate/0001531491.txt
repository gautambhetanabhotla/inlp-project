Statute	Function of sub-section (2) of section 3 of the Act is to amplify and define as to what is taken within the sweep of the term "textile undertaking" as defined in section 2(d) , which says that the expression 191 "textile undertaking" shall be deemed to include all assets, rights, leaseholds, powers, authorities and privileges of the textile company in relation to the said textile undertaking.
